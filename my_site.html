My name is Aidan Zucosky!

Lately, I have been really interested in Jean Paul Gaultier. I often think about how we use clothing as a form of self-expression. What are we trying to communicate through what we wear, and how successful are we at doing that? About a month ago, Duran Lantink presented his first collection for the house. It received a fair amount of criticism, criticism I can definitely understand. His ideas felt somewhat unfinished. At this point, I would have preferred to see a continuation of Glenn Martens’ work from his collaboration with Gaultier. However, since Martens is currently leading both Margiela and Diesel, he wasn’t able to take on Gaultier as well. Still, I am hopeful. I am excited to see how Lantink’s vision and designs evolve during his time at Jean Paul Gaultier, and I have high expectations for the upcoming couture show.
